Powerful Features

Everything you need for seamless gameplay capture across multiple monitors.

Multi-Monitor Capture

Capture gameplay across all connected displays simultaneously as a mega-canvas. Choose to save individual monitors or all at once.

Global Hotkeys

Save replays instantly with customizable global hotkeys. Direct monitor hotkeys let you skip the overlay entirely for lightning-fast saves.

NVENC Encoding

Hardware-accelerated encoding using NVIDIA NVENC keeps CPU and GPU usage minimal while maintaining excellent video quality.

Smart Cropping

Automatic per-monitor splitting using FFmpeg. Save the exact display you want without any manual editing.

Flexible Settings

Full control over buffer length, bitrate, FPS, resolution, audio sources, encoder, and output format. Tune it to your exact needs.

System Tray Integration

Runs quietly in your system tray with quick access to buffer controls, save replay, open recordings, and settings.

How It Works

Simple workflow designed for gamers who want to capture highlights effortlessly.

1

Start Buffer

Launch LuminReplay and the replay buffer starts automatically, continuously recording your gameplay.

2

Hit Your Hotkey

When something epic happens, press your save hotkey. The overlay appears to select which monitor to save.

3

Get Your Clip

Your replay is automatically cropped and saved to your designated folder. Share it instantly!
